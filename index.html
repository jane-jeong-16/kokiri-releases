Kokiri

A fast Postgres & MySQL client with an AI that actually knows your schema.

Bring your own Anthropic key — your data never leaves your machine. Only the schema is sent.

Download for macOS
Apple Silicon · free while in beta · first launch: xattr -cr /Applications/Kokiri.app

Features

✦ AI query assistantDescribe it in English; it knows your schema. BYO key, data stays local.
Postgres & MySQLOne client for both engines.
Multiple connections & tabsEach query tab can target a different database.
Browse, fastFilter, sort, paginate, edit, and combined (joined) views.
⌘I CSV importMap columns to a table and import in one step.
Content · Structure · DDLInspect any table three ways.
