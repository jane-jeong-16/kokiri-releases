Kokiri

a little database app, with a big helping of AI

Hi — I'm Jane 👋 I kept wishing my SQL client could just write the query for me — without shipping my data off to some cloud. So I made Kokiri. It talks to Postgres and MySQL, and its assistant only ever sees your schema (never your rows), using your own Anthropic key.

Download for macOS
Apple Silicon · free while it's early · first open: xattr -cr /Applications/Kokiri.app

what it does

  • Asks your database in plain English — describe it, get the SQL.
  • Postgres & MySQL, with multiple connections and tabs.
  • Browse & edit — filter, sort, joined views, CSV import (⌘I).
  • Connect your way — direct, SSH tunnel, AWS SSM, or a script.
